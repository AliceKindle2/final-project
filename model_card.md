# Limitations and Biases of the System:

The AI is limited due to its nature as a guessing game AI, having a range of numbers to correctly guess and choosing the right algorithm to guess the correct one. I noticed while playing the game mulitple times with the AI it has a set pattern it follows. It always guesses 50 at first then 25 or 75 when it recieves the first hint. After that, it typically chooses between odd numbers in it's guesses probably due to the range shortening, the computed best guess is always odd. It also cannot move forward with the guessing game until the user answers. 

# AI being Misued:

While the AI is very limited in it's role, I noticed in the early versions of the project, the AI would share the answer it was going to guess before the user would. Assuming that a user would pick the same answer as the AI and lessen the feel of the competiton, hiding it before the user guesses.

# AI Reliability:

The AI is very reliable prodiving an answer to the guessing game, only when the user enters their own answer first. At most, it takes the AI seven times to guess the correct answer. 

# Collerbation with AI:

I worked with AI to create the AI competitor. AI gave a helpful suggestion with creating the system where the AI guesses the correct answer and how it responds. The flaws Ai suggested was how the AI competitor was structured. First it would show the answer it was going to guess, then showing the range it was going to guess, and overall the structure was very distracting form the game. I fixed it with having the guess being hidden but showing the AI's history, having a calculating the best guess to fill in the empty space, and have a win result for both the AI and the user.
